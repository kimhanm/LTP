\subsection{The Lebesgue-Stieltjes Measure}
Let $F: \R \to \R$ be increasing and left continuous:
\begin{align*}
  F(x_0) = \lim_{x \to x_0^{-}}F(x) \quad \forall x_0 \in \R
\end{align*}
For $a,b$ we set
\begin{align*}
  \lambda_F[a,b) = \left\{\begin{array}{ll}
      F(b) - F(a) & \text{ if }a < b\\
    0 & \text{ otherwise}
  \end{array} \right.
\end{align*}
